Cosmos Website Design

Cosmos is a Graphic Design company. The decision to the name "Cosmos" was to leave an impression that their work is "out of this world". The process to designing this web design started with brainstorming the company and its target. After deciding the colour pallette and logo design, the home page was designed and created. The following pages and icons were vector based, and a sans serif typeface was used to keep the modern style consistent throughout the design.
