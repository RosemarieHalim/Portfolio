About

Hello, I am Rosemarie! I am half Dutch and Indonesian and was born in Canada. I speak very basic Dutch and am fluent in English. Whenever given a brain teaser I enjoy thinking outside the box and solving problems creatively. I have a thirst for knowledge and love to learn new things, methods, and techniques. When by myself I enjoy spending my time online and following along with current trends. During my free time I enjoy playing video games, hanging out at cafes or pubs, and chatting with friends. When it comes to programs, I enjoy teaching myself new approaches to Adobe Creative Cloud programs to find an efficient way to do things quickly but maintaining quality.

I completed my second year of college and consider myself a hard working student that gets the job done. I am dedicated to my work and like to go to my peers for feedback and help with brainstorming. My education at college has prepared me for the Graphic Design industry, covering a wide variety of a graphic designer's job including photography, copywriting, and coding. My educators have taught me to be thorough with my work by drawing thumbnails and getting feedback from others to find the best work.
