About

Rose is a young freelancer with a goal to create new solutions and inspire others with graphics. She is dedicated to her work and isn't afraid to improve on what she knows. Her creative journey has and continues to increase her hunger for ingenuity and creativity to push the norms of design. Her experience covers a wide variety of skills including photography, copywriting, and coding. She has learned to be thorough with her work by drawing thumbnails and getting feedback from others to create a product of the highest quality.

When given a brain teaser, she enjoys thinking outside the box and solving problems creatively and loves to learn new things, methods, and techniques to problems. When by herself, she enjoys spending her time online and following along with current trends, playing video games, and hanging out at cafes with friends and coworkers. When it comes to programs, she enjoys teaching herself new approaches to Adobe Creative Cloud to find an efficient way to do things quickly while maintaining quality. She continues to strive for quality with a pinch of fun while expanding outside of her comfort zone.
